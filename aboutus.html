About PK Holding

PK Holding is your trusted partner for high-quality building and interior solutions. We specialize in plywood, multiwood, acrylic sheets, adhesives, and modular kitchen accessories, offering a wide range of products to suit both residential and commercial needs. As authorized dealers for many of the most popular and trusted brands, we ensure that every product we deliver meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. Our mission is to provide our customers with premium materials, expert guidance, and a seamless shopping experience, helping you bring your home or project visions to life.

At PK Holding, our mission is to bring together quality, design, and affordability — ensuring every home and workspace reflects both beauty and strength.
